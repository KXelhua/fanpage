Background Information

Hangul: 방탄소년단

Origin: Seoul, South Korea (2013)

Genres: K-Pop, EDM, R&B, Electronica, Hip-Hop, Pop, Rock

Debut Anniversary: June 13, 2013

Years Active: 2013-Present

Entertainment Company: Big Hit Entertainment

Short History

They debuted on June 13, 2013 with their single "No More Dream."

Their name was conceptualized with the thought that BTS would block out stereotypes, criticisms, and expectations that aim on adolescents like bullets and protect the values and ideals of today’s adolescents.

Over the course of these last few years, BTS has become known worldwide. They have appeared in several late-night and normal talk shows, won awards in both Korea and America, broken the record for Youtube's Most-Viewed Music Video in 24 Hours, gone on worldwide tours, released a documentary (Burn the Stage) in theaters across the world, invited to give a speech in the UN General Assembly, etc .

Their fanbase is known as the BTS A.R.M.Y. (Adorable Representative M.C. of Youth)
