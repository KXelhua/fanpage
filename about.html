BTS (also known as Bangtan Sonyeondan, Bangtan Boys, Beyond the Scene, and Bulletproof Boy Scouts) is a Korean boy band composed of seven individuals: Suga, Jungkook, Jimin, RM, Jin, JHope, and V.

They debuted on June 13, 2013 with their single "No More Dream."

When they first debuted, Big Hit Entertainment, the company that BTS is signed under, was very small and unknown. Over the years, however, Big Hit Entertainment has been greatly defined by BTS' astounding and amazing worldwide success.

BTS has become significantly known over these past two years. They have gone on several worldwide tours, appeared in several late night shows, debuted their album twice in the #1 spot of the Hot 100 Billboard Charts with Love Yourself: Tear and Love Yourself: Answer, etc.
